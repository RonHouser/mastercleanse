Instructions

Step-by-step daily routine, how to make the lemonade, saltwater flush, tea timing, and safe ease-out protocol

This blank is for a video that I will make to show how to make and drink the lemonades and saltwater

Daily routine (same every day):

  • Saltwater Flush (first thing in morning): 2 tsp sea salt in 32 oz lukewarm water OR 1 tsp in 20 oz. Drink quickly. Expect elimination in ~30 min (plan time at home).
  • Lemonades (every hour, on the hour):
    • Juice from half a fresh lemon (~2 Tbsp)
    • 2 Tbsp maple syrup
    • 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
    • 8-10 oz water
    Drink 6–12+ per day (no limit — more helps if hungry/fatigued).
  • Tea
    • Smooth Move tea — last thing at night (steep 5-7 min).
    • Mint tea — optional anytime (except after Smooth Move).

Coming Off the Cleanse (Ease-Out – Minimum 2–3 Days)

  • Day 1: Fresh orange juice with pulp (drink all day), soup broth (for lunch/dinner).
  • Day 2: Orange juice, soup broth with chewed solids, leafy green salad.
  • Day 3+: Gradually return to normal diet. Start slow with carbs; avoid heavy meals initially.

Warning: Do NOT eat solid food immediately after — risk of injury to inactive digestive system.

My Daily Example

  • 5:00 AM: Wake, start bath, drink saltwater flush. Bathroom ~5:15. Bath soak.
  • Prep lunch bag: mason jars, lemons (5), squeezer, syrup, cayenne, spoons, knife.
  • Work: Make/chug lemonade on the hour (8 AM–5/6 PM). Sometimes skip to align with family dinner.
  • Drink water freely.
  • Every 5th day (or as needed): Mint tea milestone.
  • 9:00 PM: Smooth Move tea before bed.

Tip: Lunch walk (30–60 min outside, music) — becomes euphoric later in cleanse.

Expectations

What to expect day by day — the hard part, the rewards, and how it typically feels physically and mentally

Days 1–5 (Adjustment – Often Hardest)

  • Caffeine/sugar withdrawal headaches (taper off in prep week — don't cold turkey).
  • Body aches (toxin release, especially first cleanse).
  • Funky tastes/smells, dark sweat (toxins exiting).
  • Hunger mostly on-hour; drink extra lemonades if needed.
  • Longer flush times early on.

Days 6+ (Rewards Phase)

  • Joint pain disappears, steady energy, euphoria.
  • Lemonades feel refreshing/invigorating.
  • Fuzzy tongue/teeth — brush regularly.
  • Boredom with taste — normal; think about post-cleanse foods.

Weight loss: ~1 lb/day average after initial water/waste drop. Not a fast — hunger managed with more lemonade.
